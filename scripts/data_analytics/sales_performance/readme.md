## 📊 Sales Performance Over Time

### 📌 Objective

Analyze how sales performance evolves over time by tracking key business metrics such as total sales, customer count, and quantity sold on a monthly basis.

---


### 📈 Metrics Used

* **Total Sales** → Overall revenue generated per month
* **Total Customers** → Number of unique customers per month
* **Total Quantity** → Total items sold per month

---

### 📊 Tableau Visualization

![Sales Performance Dashboard](docs/data_analysis_dashboard/sales_performance_over_time.png)

---

### 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales trends can be observed across different months and years
* Helps identify **peak sales periods** and **low-performing months**
* Customer growth trends can indicate business expansion or decline
* Quantity sold helps understand product demand over time

---

### 🧠 Business Value

This analysis helps stakeholders to:

* Make **data-driven decisions** on sales strategies
* Identify **seasonality patterns**
* Plan **inventory and marketing campaigns**
* Track **customer engagement over time**
